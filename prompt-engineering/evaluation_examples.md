# AI Response Evaluation

When evaluating LLM responses, reviewers typically assess several dimensions.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Accuracy
- Completeness
- Relevance
- Clarity
- Safety
- Factual Consistency

---

## Example

### Prompt

Summarize the benefits of renewable energy.

### Good Response

Renewable energy sources such as solar and wind reduce greenhouse gas emissions, improve energy security, and support sustainable development.

### Poor Response

Renewable energy is always free and has no disadvantages.

### Why?

The poor response contains factual inaccuracies and is misleading.
